Responsible Social Media Use Checklist
- Do’s
* Think before posting—consider how it may affect your reputation. 
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
* Maintain a positive and respectful tone in comments and posts.
* Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your content.

-Don’ts
* Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or hate speech 
* Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown attachments. 
* Don’t overshare your location or daily activities in real-time.
* Don’t post inappropriate or unprofessional content.
