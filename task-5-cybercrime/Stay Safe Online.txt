Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

Prevention Tips
* Use strong, unique passwords for each account (avoid using the same password everywhere) 
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps 
* Keep your devices updated with the latest software and security patches
* Always check for HTTPS and correct website URLs before entering login details 
* Do not click on suspicious links received via email, SMS, or WhatsApp 
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts (banking, email, etc.)
* Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank
* Double-check UPI payment requests—receiving money does NOT require entering your PIN

If Something Goes Wrong
* Immediately report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (especially for financial fraud) 
* Inform your bank quickly to try and block or reverse transactions
